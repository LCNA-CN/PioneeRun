About PioneeRun / 关于 PioneeRun

PioneeRun is a 48-hour creative tech marathon bringing together developers, designers, and storytellers from around the city.
PioneeRun 是一场为期48小时的创意技术马拉松，汇聚全市开发者、设计师和叙事者。
